About Me

Why SIUGO?

SIUGO is a nickname for Gloria. But there are so many Glorias' out there and I want to be unique. And as a mobile makeup artist and hair stylist who specializes in weddings, each bride should be unique.

This is my vision and passion and it is what SIUGO represents - to enhance your unique natural beauty.

My area of service covers Metro Vancouver and I am available for destination weddings. I am fluent in English, Cantonese and Mandarin.
